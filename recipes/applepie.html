Apple pie

This is the best apple pie in the world. It is a recipe from the Cordon Bleu.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups thinly sliced peeled tart apples
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • Pastry for double-crust pie (9 inches)
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Sugar

Directions

  1. In a large bowl, toss apples with lemon juice. Combine sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg and salt; add to apples and toss well to coat. Spoon into a 1-gallon freezer bag. Seal, label and freeze, laying bag flat in freezer.
  2. To serve, thaw filling in refrigerator overnight. Place in a greased 9-in. pie plate. Dot with butter. Roll out pastry to fit top of pie; place over filling. Trim, seal and flute edges. Cut slits in pastry. Sprinkle with sugar. Cover edges loosely with foil.
  3. Bake at 375Â° for 25 minutes. Remove foil and bake 20-25 minutes longer or until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly. Cool on a wire rack.
